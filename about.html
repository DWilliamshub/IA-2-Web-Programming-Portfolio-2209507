About CaribWords

Preserving our culture, empowering our authors, and celebrating the Caribbean story.

Our Mission

CaribWords was created to make Caribbean literature accessible to readers everywhere. Our goal is to provide a digital platform where local authors, poets, and storytellers can share their work with students, teachers, and book lovers around the world.

What We Offer

  • Instant downloads of Caribbean e-books in PDF and EPUB formats.
  • Thematic collections featuring folklore, youth fiction, and poetry.
  • Biographies and highlights of local authors and educators.
  • A growing community of readers who love Caribbean storytelling.
